Hello!
I'm Valeria, though you can simply call me Vale. Welcome to my portfolio, where I showcase my expertise as an Interactive Graphic Designer. The content below offers a glimpse into my proficiency in UI/UX design, prototyping, and coding for both desktop and mobile websites, as well as endeavors in crafting visual and brand identities. Drawing inspiration from my background in mural painting, I aim to seamlessly merge traditional artistic creativity into my design work. My design philosophy revolves around innovative problem-solving through creativity. Feel free to explore websites I have fully coded or see the process behind it!
